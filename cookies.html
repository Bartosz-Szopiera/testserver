Cookies

General Information

  1. Purpose

    A cookie consisits of a piece of text sent by a web server to a web browser, and stored by the browser. The information may or may not be sent back to the server with future requests (your attempts to communicate with the server).

    Cookies make possible:

    • to tell your browsing sessions apart,

    • remember some of your choices and not have you make them again with each visit (like to allow cookies).

  2. Main cookie types

    Session cookie

    Cookie is deleted as soon as you close your browser (it may be reinstantiated if you restore previous session on next browser start-up).

    Persistent cookie

    Cookie is stored on you computer until it is deleted, or reaches its expiration date.

  3. Privacy and Security

    Cookies stored by this website do not save any personal or sensitive data.
